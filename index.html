About

Hey, I'm Young Fenimore Lee. I attend Adlai E. Stevenson High School in Lincolnshire, Illinois.

I run my school's Ethics Bowl club and team at SHS, where we frame moral dilemmas through either hypothetical or real scenarios embedded in case studies. I run the club alongside the illustrious and glorious Mr. Clayton Duba. We also read philosophical works of fiction as a fun aside, such as Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep? I plan on us reading No Exit and some other Jean-Paul Sartre plays, too, (I recommend The Respectful Prostitute for existential philosophy with a side order of a glaring critique of American racism) since he is possibly one of my favorite philosophers. Aside from, of course, John Rawls. Aside from Ethics Bowl, I plan to participate in next year's American Philosophy Olympiad, the United States olympiad for qualifying for the International Philosophy Olympiad.

I was actually introduced to a lot of philosophy through my other major passion, quizbowl. I play on the SHS quizbowl team alongside brilliant generalist Ali Saeed, history aficionados Kazuma Shiminaka and Marco Laudati, and Fine Arts specialist Nathan Cha. I am personally responsible for studying RMP (Religion, Mythology, Philosophy), Fine Arts (mainly concerning classical music, jazz history, art history, and architecture), and whatever else I can manage to study. Our team is ranked 18th on this year's HS QB Rank, and we hope to do well at this year's national level competitions (NSC in Chicago, Illinois and HSNCT in Dallas, Texas) in May and July.

I solve Rubik's Cubes competitively at World Cube Association sanctioned competitions in the midwest region. I have an 11 second unofficial 3x3 average and a much worse in-competition average. I also have a 2x2 average of around 4 seconds, and average around 2 minutes for solving a 3x3 blindfolded.

Other of my interests include attending hackathons like MHacks and Codeday, playing the clarinet in my school's Honor Band, and playing the cello in the Chicago Youth Symphony Orchestra. So yeah, that's me. I also have a blog, where I talk about my personal philosophy of life, along with other stuff. Contact me through the links at the bottom of the page if you want/need to, for whatever reason, and thanks for taking a look around.
